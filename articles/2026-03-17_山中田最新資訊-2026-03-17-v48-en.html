Strategic Recommendations

1. Strengthen Digital Capabilities
Invest in cybersecurity measures and cloud-based collaboration tools. Remote and hybrid work models are becoming permanent fixtures, requiring robust digital infrastructure.

2. Diversify Supply Chain Networks
Reduce dependency on single-source suppliers by developing relationships with multiple vendors. This approach mitigates risk and improves operational resilience.

4. Develop Local Talent Pipeline
